01 — MESSAGE

Message from Managing Director

Murata Electronics (Thailand) was established as the first Japanese company around this region. Since the start of operation, we succeed to expand our business continuously.

Murata has a strong corporate philosophy, so-called "SHAZE". It was created by our founder and consists of "enhancing technology and skills", "applying scientific approach" and so on. "SHAZE" is our fundamentals of innovation.

Our market keeps drastic changing. Murata is also required to be a strong innovator under such circumstances. We share and practice "SHAZE" deeply and seriously — it is only the way to succeed and be an innovator.

Murata is No.1 company of electronic components in the world. Murata should contribute to the development of society by fulfilling its responsibilities as a good global corporate citizen. We also show such responsibilities and grow together with employees, local government and communities for long time.

Murata Electronics (Thailand)., Ltd. manufactures high quality electronic components used in both industrial and consumer electronic equipment. With the classification of our products by their characteristics, we meet the growing demand for advanced functions and added value in electronic devices by dividing them into two categories according to their applications and usage.

Responsible operations We recognize the sensitive nature of our business concerning the environment. Keeping in line with the Murata Corporate Environment Policy. We are committed to be responsible for monitoring all of environment impacts of our business. We will make every endeavour to develop and manufacture products which are environmentally responsible and continually improve our environmental performance with regard to pollution prevention.

Our strategy for future development is based on these two categories, together with Responsible Operations.

Environmental, Occupational Health & Safety Policy

We recognize the sensitive nature of our business toward the environment. Keeping in line with Murata Corporate Policy we commit to be responsible for monitoring all impacts from our business, improving our environmental performance upon pollution prevention continually with considering a life cycle perspective.

1

To COMPLY — Comply with relevant and applicable Environment, Occupational health, Safety, and Energy conservation laws, regulations, code of practices in Thailand and obligations of interested parties with regards to our business.

2

To AWARE — Raise individual awareness as well as develop a corporate entity on Environment, Occupational health, Safety responsibilities and Energy conservation, thereby inculcating propriety into each employee.

3

To ENCOURAGE — Promote optimal, efficient use of resources and energy. At the same time, encourage activities on the elimination of waste, prevention and control minimum impact on our environment.

4

To BUILD — Create Environment, Occupational health, Safety, and Energy conservation awareness in workplace. By consulting, participating in risk assessment, improve, developing implementing and maintaining management systems according to standard management of Environment, Occupational health, Safety, and Energy conservation to achieve continuous improvement.
